Privacy Policy

MazerDaze · Last updated: April 24, 2026

1. Information We Collect

MazerDaze collects only the information necessary to provide automated messaging services. This includes phone numbers of recipients who have consented to receive messages, and message content necessary to deliver notifications.

2. How We Use Information

Phone numbers and contact information are used solely to deliver automated notifications, reminders, and alerts to consenting recipients. We do not use this information for marketing purposes or share it with third parties.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share personal information with third parties. Message delivery is handled through Twilio, Inc., which processes message data in accordance with their privacy policy.

4. Data Retention

Contact information is retained only as long as necessary to provide the messaging service. Recipients may request deletion of their information at any time by contacting us.

5. Opt-Out

Recipients may opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. Opt-out requests are processed immediately.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at scrumbot2000@gmail.com or droiddarm@gmail.com.
