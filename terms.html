MazerDaze provides automated SMS notifications, reminders, and alerts to consenting recipients via a 10-digit long code (10DLC) phone number. Messages include household reminders, schedule notifications, and coordination alerts.

2. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on activity and scheduled events. Recipients may receive up to several messages per day depending on configured alerts and reminders.

3. Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Recipients are responsible for any charges from their mobile carrier.

4. Opt-In

Recipients must provide explicit consent before receiving messages from MazerDaze. Consent is obtained directly and personally by the account owner prior to sending any messages.

5. Opt-Out

Recipients may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, no further messages will be sent. To re-subscribe, reply START.
